Cost of Banking 2018

Paul Gubbins, Francis Gwer, Edoardo Totolo, Jack Odero
December 2019
PDF

Abstract

This report is the second in a series of studies that measures the cost of banking services in Kenya and is focused on deposit accounts offered by the 11 banks that cumulatively control 97% of the total number of deposit accounts in Kenya as at December 2017.
